Additionally, I have enhanced my public speaking and communication skills through my work as a campus tour guide and my continued leadership in the organization, all while building upon my reporting and research skills through the journalism program in College Park and the internships I’ve held.

My course load includes news writing and reporting for multi-media and broadcast, social engagement and analytics, copyediting and videography. Open to opportunities involving audio and podcast reporting, editing or social media engagement.
